Meal Planner

Automated weekly meal planning and grocery cart management system

About Meal Planner

An intelligent meal planning system that integrates with your calendar, generates personalized meal plans using AI, and automatically populates your grocery cart. Perfect for busy families who want to streamline their weekly meal preparation.

Key Features

  • 📅 Calendar Integration - Reads your iCloud calendar to plan around events
  • 🧠 AI-Powered Planning - Uses Claude AI to generate personalized meal suggestions
  • 🛒 Automated Shopping - Populates your Kroger cart with needed ingredients
  • 📱 SMS Notifications - Sends plan updates and grocery alerts to your phone
  • 📚 Learning System - Remembers preferences and avoids recent meals
  • 👥 Multi-User Support - Works for couples and families

How It Works

1. Saturday Morning

System reads your calendar and generates a draft meal plan for the upcoming week

2. Review & Confirm

Receive SMS notification, review the plan, and confirm your preferences

3. Grocery List Creation

Automatically builds shopping list and adds items to your Kroger cart

4. Shop & Cook

Pick up groceries or schedule delivery, then follow your personalized meal plan

Technology

Built using GitHub Actions for automation, integrating with Claude AI, iCloud Calendar, Kroger API, and Twilio SMS. Fully cloud-hosted with no local hardware requirements.
